Payment

Payment is made in advance in the amount of 100% of the order amount for all types of services.


Payment can be made by:
- A transfer to card;
- A cash payment at a meeting.


There are discounts for wholesale orders.

Shipping

Shipping of the order can be carried out by:
- Nova Poshta (customer pays for the shipping);
- Shipping in Ivano-Frankivsk (free).


The cost of shipping by Nova Poshta is determined by the carrier's tariffs and is paid by the client upon receipt of the order. Shipping time in Ukraine 1-3 days.


Shipping outside Ukraine is possible and discussed directly with the client.
