About Technology

Applying rhinestones is a process of customizing clothes that imitate gems. Rhinestones are made of lead glass with glue. Rhinestones can be applied to t-shirts, sweatshirts, jeans and other clothing with flax, wool, silk and leather. The machine allows you to put rhinestones on clothes in the form of drawings, logos or texts.

The principle of applying rhinestones is simple: the image is created using colored stones, which are applied to the fabric with a thermal press. During applying, the thermopress warms the rhinestones with ultrasound to 200 degrees Celsius, melts the glue and sticks the stones in the appropriate places. Thus enhances product quality and durability.
