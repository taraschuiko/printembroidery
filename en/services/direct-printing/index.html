About Technology

Direct digital printing on clothing (Direct to Garment, DTG) is a relatively new technology of printing an image on fabric, which makes it possible to turn t-shirts, sweatshirts, hoodies and other clothes into exclusive, stylish clothes. Printing is performed by a printer that directly prints ink on the fabric using inkjet technology.

For printing, we use water-based Dupont non-toxic inks that are environmentally friendly. Therefore, clothing with such a print is perfect for children. Direct digital printing on T-shirts is not limited to the number of colors, so multi-color printing or printing photos will not be a problem. You can create unique designs for clothes of any complexity, with sizes up to 420 x 594 mm.
