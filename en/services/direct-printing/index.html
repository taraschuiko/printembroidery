Methods of printing on fabrics

Direct digital printing on fabrics

Direct digital printing(Direct to Garment, DTG) — is new printing technology that allow you to transform t-shirst, sweaters into exclusive, stylish clothes. Printing is performed by a printer that directly prints ink on the fabric using liquid inkjet technology.

For printing, we use non-toxic Dupont water-based inks that are eco-friendly. Therefore, clothes with this print are great for children. Direct digital printing on T-shirts is not limited by the number of colors, so multicolor or photo printing will not be a problem. You can create unique designs for clothing of any complexity, up to 420 x 594 mm in size.

Heat transfer printing

Heat transfer printing is a printing method by which images are applied to a thermal transfer film and then transferred to t-shirts, sweaters, hoodie and other clothing. The transfer and fixing is due to the action of high temperature. The advantage of heat transfer printing is that the ink is firmly fixed to the printed material, allowing long wearing of clothing.

The thermotransfer film is ideal for application to synthetic, mixed and natural fabrics. With the help of a thermal film it is possible to apply logos, images, inscriptions on clothes, flags, uniforms, overalls, bedding, specials textiles, etc. As a result of thermal transfer printing, your image can be single-color, multi-color, or with special effects (glare or glow).
