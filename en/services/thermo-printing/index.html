About Technology

Heat transfer printing is a printing method with which the image is applied to heat transfer printing film and then transferred to t-shirts, sweatshirts, hoodies and other clothes. Transfer occurs through action of high temperature. The advantage of heat transfer printing is that the ink is firmly fixed on the printed material as well This allows you to wear clothes for a long time.

Heat transfer film is ideal for applying to synthetic, mixed and natural fabrics. Using a heat transfer film, you can apply logos, images, text on clothing, flags, uniforms, clothing, special textiles, etc. As a result of using thermal transfer printing, your image can be one-color, multi-color or with special effects (glare or night glow).
