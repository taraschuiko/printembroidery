About Technology

Machine embroidery is the best way to put logos or emblems on clothes. However, with the help of embroidery you can apply various illustrations or drawings. Machine embroidery can be applied to any fabric and looks more prestigious than digital or thermal transfer printing. Embroidery on clothes creates a bright, clear and stable image that will never crack or wipe out; which is able to withstand frequent washing and hot sunshine.

The principle of machine embroidery is similar to the principle of hand-made: the image is created with colored threads, which are applied to the fabric with a needle. But, unlike hand embroidery, machine embroidery is performed on a special embroidery machine, which has a large number of needles, which move with great speed. The designer creates a sketch of the drawing and transfers it to the computer that controls the embroidery machine.
